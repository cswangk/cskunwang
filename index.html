Brief Biography: Kun Wang received two Ph.D. degrees from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China in 2009 and from the University of Aizu, Japan in 2018, respectively, both in Computer Science. I was a Postdoc Fellow in UCLA, USA from 2013 to 2015, and a Research Fellow in the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong, from 2017 to 2018. He is currently a Research Fellow in UCLA.
I am the recipient of Best Paper Award at IEEE GLOBECOM16. I serve as Associate Editor of IEEE Access, Editor of Journal of Network and Computer Applications, and Guest Editors of IEEE Network, IEEE Access, Future Generation Computer Systems, Peer-to-Peer Networking and Applications, and Journal of Internet Technology.
